# Extensions

Extensions continue stacking thirds above the 7th.

| Name | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| 9 | 1 3 5 ♭7 9 |
| maj9 | 1 3 5 7 9 |
| m9 | 1 ♭3 5 ♭7 9 |
| 11 | 1 3 5 ♭7 9 11 |
| 13 | 1 3 5 ♭7 9 11 13 |

Equivalent scale degrees:

```text
9  = 2
11 = 4
13 = 6
```

One octave higher.

# Chords Built From a Major Scale

Using C major:

```text
C D E F G A B
```

Build a triad by taking every other note:

```text
1-3-5
2-4-6
3-5-7
...
```

| Degree | Notes | Chord |
|----------|----------|----------|
| I | C E G | Major |
| ii | D F A | Minor |
| iii | E G B | Minor |
| IV | F A C | Major |
| V | G B D | Major |
| vi | A C E | Minor |
| vii° | B D F | Diminished |

Pattern:

```text
I      Major
ii     Minor
iii    Minor
IV     Major
V      Major
vi     Minor
vii°   Diminished
```

# Roman Numeral Convention

| Symbol | Meaning |
|----------|----------|
| I | Major chord |
| ii | Minor chord |
| vii° | Diminished chord |
| V7 | Dominant seventh |
| ♭VII | Flat-seven major chord |
| #iv° | Sharp-four diminished chord |

Uppercase = Major

Lowercase = Minor

Degree sign (°) = Diminished

# Quick Decoder Ring

Start with a major chord:

```text
1 3 5
```

Then apply modifications:

| Symbol | Change |
|----------|----------|
| m | Lower 3rd |
| dim | Lower 3rd and 5th |
| aug | Raise 5th |
| 7 | Lower 7th |
| maj7 | Natural 7th |
| sus2 | Replace 3rd with 2nd |
| sus4 | Replace 3rd with 4th |
| add9 | Add 9 |
| ♭5 | Lower 5th |
| ♯5 | Raise 5th |
| ♭9 | Lower 9th |
| ♯9 | Raise 9th |

Examples:

```text
C        = 1 3 5
Cm       = 1 ♭3 5
Cdim     = 1 ♭3 ♭5
Caug     = 1 3 ♯5

Cmaj7    = 1 3 5 7
C7       = 1 3 5 ♭7
Cm7      = 1 ♭3 5 ♭7
Cm7♭5    = 1 ♭3 ♭5 ♭7
C°7      = 1 ♭3 ♭5 ♭♭7

Csus2    = 1 2 5
Csus4    = 1 4 5
Cadd9    = 1 3 5 9
```

```text

```

# What Each Chord Tone Does

| Tone | Role |
|--------|--------|
| Root (1) | Establishes the harmonic center ("where we are") |
| Third (3 or ♭3) | Determines major vs. minor character |
| Fifth (5) | Reinforces stability; often optional |
| Seventh (7 or ♭7) | Adds tension, color, and direction |
| Ninth (9) | Adds openness, richness, sophistication |
| Eleventh (11) | Adds suspension, ambiguity, spaciousness |
| Thirteenth (13) | Adds warmth and fullness |

Rule of thumb:

```text
Root     = Identity
Third    = Character
Fifth    = Support
Seventh  = Motion
9/11/13  = Color
```
