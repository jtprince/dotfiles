
DTIMS - Drift Tube Ion Mobility Spectrometry (Classic static field; directly measures physical size)
TWIMS - Traveling Wave Ion Mobility Spectrometry (Dynamic moving waves; avoids ion loss)
TIMS  - Trapped Ion Mobility Spectrometry (Stationary gas-balance trap; high resolution in tiny space)
cIM   - Cyclic Ion Mobility (Closed circular loop; infinite path length for ultra-high resolution)
SLIM  - Structures for Lossless Ion Manipulations (Winding serpentine board; endless paths without loss)DMS   - Differential Mobility Spectrometry (Perpendicular field filter; screens ions by shape change)
