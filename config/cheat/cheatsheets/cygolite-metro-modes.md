### Lock mode
rapid flashing when press power, then it's locked
to unlock: hold button for 6 seconds
to lock: turn on, then let go, and then press down for 6 seconds

### Nighttime Modes (default)
* boost (also, a double press anytime gets boost mode, one press to exit)
* high
* medium low
* stady pulse

### Daytime (aka Flashing) Modes

w/ light off, press power for 2 seconds. Then power to scroll through modes

* DayLightning
* Triple Flash
* Zoom
* Walking

Light starts in last mode used
